**Sprint 14 foundation.** Codify the Obs Loop as ADR 0030.

- A **slow** (hours/daily), **per-integration** scheduled detector loop producing **Observations** (ISE's own judgement where a source has no detection layer) — distinct from Alerts (a source's own detection, which ISE forwards).
- **Deterministic detectors + context-driven suppression first; AI reserved for genuine novelty** (the cost discipline).
- Captures a **baseline of record** ("normal") and validates the estate knowledge base against reality — **drift → low-severity Observations**.
- **Retires the 15-/30-min summarise/analyse timers** (the Obs Loop replaces them).

*No screen (ADR).*
