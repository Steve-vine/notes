Pin how the immutability of system taxonomies (Type, Task Status, Project Status) is enforced — ADR 0005 says they're "not user-deletable or reshape-able" but doesn't say how, and `taxonomies.yaml` is a hand-editable file in the vault.

**Decision (recommending A):** system taxonomies are **app-owned** (compiled-in constants); `taxonomies.yaml` holds **user-defined taxonomies only**. The loader merges built-ins (from code) with user defs (from file) and ignores any `system: true` entries in the file. Removes the corruption surface structurally rather than detecting/repairing it.
