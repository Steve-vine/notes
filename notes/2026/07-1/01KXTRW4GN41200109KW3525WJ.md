**Sprint 11 (spine).** The durable, human-owned **Incident**, replacing the 1:1 `promotion.py` Finding→Issue mapping.

**Delivered (accepted slice, on staging — PR #101):** the load-bearing ADR 0025 behaviour on the existing `issue` table + status vocabulary.
- The durable record no longer flaps with its signal: a cleared signal recovers but never auto-resolves the incident (resolution is human-owned).
- Resolving an incident **cascades** its signal to `resolved` (`resolve_incident_signals`).
- Only a **genuine recurrence** (signal went away and came back → `recurring`) reactivates a resolved incident; a still-firing signal cannot bounce a human's resolution back open.
