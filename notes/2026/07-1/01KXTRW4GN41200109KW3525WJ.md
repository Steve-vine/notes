**Sprint 11 (spine).** The durable, human-owned **Incident**, replacing the 1:1 `promotion.py` Finding→Issue mapping.

- State machine: New/Active/Resolved/Reactivated/Closed (Closed auto after *x*-days-quiet; recurrence after Closed = new Incident; correlation key persists on Resolved-but-not-Closed so a recurrence reattaches).
- **Ingest correlation:** a signal opens or attaches to at most one *open* Incident by stable key; recovered-and-re-triggered re-attaches rather than duplicating.
- **Opened by:** Alert (≥ threshold); severe high-confidence Observation (≥ threshold); or a human (manual, from any signal).
- Resolving an Incident cascades its signals to Resolved.
- **Merge** of related Incidents is AI-proposed / human-confirmed (never automatic).

Alembic migration; supersede `promotion.py`. Integration tests. Depends on the Signal models + the ADR.
