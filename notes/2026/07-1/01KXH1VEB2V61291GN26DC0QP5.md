## Message history: store prose, NOT pydantic-ai `ModelMessage` JSON

Persisting the transcript blob and replaying it looks tempting and is wrong, for two reasons:

1. **Redaction is destructive.** ADR 0010 redacts everything persisted. Replaying a redacted transcript feeds `[REDACTED]` back to the model as if it were fact.
2. **Tool payloads are capped at 40k chars each** (`bound_payload`). Replaying full transcripts means turn 5 re-sends every slice payload the conversation ever read. Context and cost explode.

So: rebuild history as plain `ModelRequest(UserPromptPart)` / `ModelResponse(TextPart)` pairs from the message rows, windowed to the last N turns (`ai_chat_history_turns`, default ~12). Tool calls are dropped from history; the model re-calls tools if it needs the data.

**The cost, stated plainly:** the model loses "what I already looked up" and may redundantly re-read. That is the price of a history that survives redaction, and it is the right trade.
