Create the 'Sticky' note type.  A special note type for Workspaces, although still visible as normal notes in Browse.  In Workspaces they appear as a Post-it or Sticky note, with the text visible, and movable like icons.  

Sticky notes should be able to be edited, resized, deleted, moved and the colour changed from a palette of main colours.  They should have a title, body and taxonomies like all notes, but only the title and body are visible in Workspaces.  In Memo view they look and act like other notes but appear in their own Type.
