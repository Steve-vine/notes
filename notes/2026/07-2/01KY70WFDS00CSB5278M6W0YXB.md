(Spec clarified in chat, recorded for the record.)

A menu icon at the far right of the Dashboard/Browse/Kanban tab strip, visible only while the Kanban board is visible (not when a card's contents are shown). The menu has two sections with tickable subitems, one tick per section:

* **Order by** — Priority, Date Created, Date Due, Assignee, Manual
* **Sort Order** — Ascending, Descending

These determine the within-column card sort order. When Manual is selected, cards can be dragged up and down; while dragging, a horizontal line shows between cards (and at the top/bottom of the stack) to indicate where the card would be placed on release.
