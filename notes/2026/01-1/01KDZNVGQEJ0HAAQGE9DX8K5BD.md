### Trust Criteria

- **Security (Mandatory):** Also known as the "Common Criteria," this protects against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of information. It includes access controls, change management, and monitoring.
- **Availability:** Ensures systems, products, or services are available for operation and use as agreed upon (e.g., in SLAs).
- **Processing Integrity:** Confirms system processing is complete, valid, accurate, timely, and authorized (e.g., in data processing or transactions).
- **Confidentiality:** Protects sensitive information (e.g., intellectual property, financial data) by restricting access to authorized users.
- **Privacy:** Addresses the collection, use, retention, disclosure, and disposal of personal information in accordance with privacy notices.
