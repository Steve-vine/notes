Benefits of SSO

✅ Centralised authentication - 1 Password to remember
❌ Centralised authorisation - Role based access control
❌ Centralised auditing - record all activity in a central audit log
❌ Persistent login session, login once and stay logged in - centrally issued token
❌ Simplified Management (one identity to manage), faster onboarding, no orphaned accounts
❌ Disable one account to instantly revoke access to all systems

## What Makes True SSO

1. A formal authentication protocol (like SAML or OIDC)
2. Explicit identity assertions about who the user is
3. Centralized session management
4. Standardized user attributes and claims
5. Coordinated logout capabilities
